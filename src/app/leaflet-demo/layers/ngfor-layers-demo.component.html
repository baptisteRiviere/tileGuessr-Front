Layers and *ngFor

You can also use *ngFor or *ngIf to show/hide individual layers by nesting them inside the map element. In this case, you can use [leafletLayer] to bind an individual layer. 

<div leaflet style="height: 300px;" [leafletOptions]="options">
	<div *ngFor="let m of markers" [leafletLayer]="m"></div>
</div>
